## Preamble

This license is designed to ensure that open source software remains freely available to individuals, small organizations, and nonprofits, while requiring larger commercial entities to contribute back to the sustainability of the project.

## Definitions

- **"Small Organization"**: An entity with fewer than 50 employees AND less than $5 million USD in annual revenue.
- **"Large Commercial Entity"**: An entity with 50 or more employees OR $5 million USD or more in annual revenue.
- **"Nonprofit"**: An organization recognized as tax-exempt under applicable law, or operating primarily for charitable, educational, or public benefit purposes without distributing profits to owners or shareholders.
